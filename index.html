Privacy Policy

Workly  ·  Last updated: May 2026

Workly ("we", "our", or "the app") is a work-hours tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using Workly, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app's features:

  • Work records — shift dates, hours worked, overtime, day type (regular / weekend / holiday), notes, and optional photos you attach to a record.
  • Salary configuration — base salary, hourly rate, and overtime multipliers you enter in the app. These are stored locally on your device.
  • Account information — if you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address from those providers solely to identify your cloud backup.
  • Device locale — your device's region setting is read once at startup to look up local public holidays automatically. It is not stored or transmitted independently.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your work records and calculate salary summaries.
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase cloud backup (only when you are signed in and initiate a backup).
  • To fetch public holiday data for your region from third-party APIs (Nager.Date and Calendarific) so the app can highlight holidays on the calendar.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or any third party for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

  • Local storage — all work records and settings are stored in a local SQLite database on your device. This data stays on your device unless you choose to back it up.
  • Cloud backup — when you sign in and use the backup feature, your records are encrypted in transit and stored in Google Firebase Firestore under your account. You can delete this backup at any time from the app.
  • Photos — photos attached to records are stored locally on your device and, if backup is enabled, uploaded to Firebase Storage under your account.

4. Third-Party Services

Workly integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage)
  • Google Google Sign-In
  • Apple Sign in with Apple
  • API Nager.Date — public holiday data (no account required)
  • API Calendarific — public holiday data (no account required)

5. Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or delete a record manually. Cloud backup data is retained until you delete it from the app or delete your account. We do not retain any data on our own servers.

6. Your Rights

  • Access — all your data is visible directly in the app.
  • Delete local data — uninstall the app or delete individual records from within the app.
  • Delete cloud data — use the "Delete Backup" option in the app, or contact us and we will delete it for you.
  • Sign out — signing out stops any further cloud sync. Local data is not affected.

7. Children's Privacy

Workly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
